Delete Your Account – StreetJams

If you would like to delete your StreetJams account and associated data, you can do so using one of the methods below.

Option 1: In-App

You can delete your account directly within the StreetJams app by going to:

Profile → Delete Account

Option 2: Request via Email

You can also request account deletion by contacting us at:

Email: hello@streetjams.app

Please include your account email in the request.

Data Deletion

When your account is deleted:

  • Your profile and personal data will be permanently removed
  • User-generated content may be deleted

Deletion requests are processed within 3 days.
